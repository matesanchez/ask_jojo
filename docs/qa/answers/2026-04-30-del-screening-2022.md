## Answer

DEL screening at Nurix in 2022 ran out of the [[del-screening|DEL Screening Program]], which was tracked under the label "DS Projects" through the first half of the year and renamed to "DSA Projects" (DEL Screen and Analysis) by June 2022 [[del-screening|del-screening]]. The activity was organized around per-target campaigns indexed C1, C2, C3 and onward, where each campaign represents one selection round against one construct or paired domain set. Several targets had multiple campaigns in the same year because repeat screens against newly available library subsets or updated constructs were common.

The 2022 queue itself was set in March 2022 and is captured in two artifacts authored by Jose Santos: the compact taxonomy in `DEL_queue_2022.pptx` (created 2022-03-18) and the Gantt-style operational view in `DEL-Protein-Screen-2022.pptx` (created 2022-04-26, modified 2022-08-22) [[2022-del-screen-queue|2022-del-screen-queue]]. The taxonomy organized the year's screens into a two-by-two grid: High Complexity versus Low Complexity along one axis, and June 1, 2022 versus end-of-year delivery along the other. The complexity classification is a scheduling decision rather than a biology judgment — complex screens were placed earlier so that the second half of the year had capacity for follow-up work [[2022-del-screen-queue|2022-del-screen-queue]].

The High Complexity slate for June 1, 2022 delivery was: Promiscuity Panel ([[nrx09-promiscuity-panel|NRX09]]), CRAF, ADAR, IRF5, NR4A1, STS1. The High Complexity slate for end-of-year delivery was: GLD07, AURKA/MYCN, EWS-FLI1, SIAH1. The Low Complexity slate for June 1 delivery was: CBL-B and B-Catenin. The Low Complexity slate for end-of-year delivery was: HPK1, BRAF, CISH, Tet-2, SGC Ligases, Internal Ligases, Ligandability Panel, SMARCA2, STAT6, IRAK4. A trailing "Kinase panel (?)" item marked a proposed but unconfirmed addition [[2022-del-screen-queue|2022-del-screen-queue]].

The August 2022 DSA Update operationalized the queue and reported Hit Resynthesis dates for 20 of the year's campaigns by that snapshot, including NR4A1 C3 on 2022-06-24, IRF5 C4 on 2022-07-20, GRWD1 C3 on 2022-09-26, FEM1C C1 and C2 on 2022-09-22, STS1 C1, UBR1 C1, and SMARCA2 C2 all on 2022-09-10, UBR1 C2, SIAH1 C3, and SIAH1 C4 on 2022-10-01, and the IAP panel (BIRC2, BIRC3, XIAP) C3 on 2022-11-01 [[2022-del-screen-queue|2022-del-screen-queue]]. The DSA Update from 2022-08-18 also stated the program intent for the remainder of the year, which was to focus heavily on ligases including the targets in the "ligandability" effort [[del-screening|del-screening]]. Some targets on the Queue 2022 slate are not reflected in the August 2022 snapshot, which the wiki interprets as either later execution or deferral rather than as a positive reorganization decision.

The Q4 2022 screening budget decision and the DSA early-discovery cadence are companion records to the queue and capture the resourcing and meeting cadence that surrounded it [[q4-2022-screening-budget|q4-2022-screening-budget]] [[dsa-early-discovery-cadence-2022|dsa-early-discovery-cadence-2022]]. Together with the queue page they form the wiki's authoritative 2022 snapshot. None of these pages are appropriate to quote as *current* queue guidance; for current campaign state the program page directs readers to the DSA Projects workbook as the living tracker [[del-screening|del-screening]].
